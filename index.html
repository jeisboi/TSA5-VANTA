Son Goku


100% Saiyan

A Saiyan baby sent to Earth in a pod to conquer the planet. However, a bump on the head alters his memory, turning him into a kindhearted and carefree boy. Raised by Grandpa Gohan, Goku becomes a martial arts prodigy known for his pure heart and unwavering determination. He constantly strives to become stronger, pushing himself through intense training and epic battles. Goku unlocks legendary transformations like Super Saiyan and achieves god-like power throughout the series.Primarily relies on hand-to-hand combat, known for his incredible speed and reflexes. Utilizes signature techniques like the Kamehameha wave, a powerful energy blast. Goku is a versatile fighter, adapting his tactics and learning new techniques throughout his journey.

Son Gohan


Saiyan-Human

The studious son of Goku, Gohan possesses immense potential as a fighter but initially prefers a peaceful life. However, when faced with threats like Cell and Majin Buu, he unleashes extraordinary power, becoming the first Super Saiyan 2. Gohan struggles to balance his scholarly pursuits with his Saiyan heritage but ultimately becomes a powerful defender of Earth. Possesses a more strategic fighting style than Goku, utilizing a combination of martial arts and powerful energy blasts. Gohan's hidden power allows him to surpass even his father in terms of raw power at times.

Picollo


Namekian

Originally a cunning and ruthless Namekian warrior sent to Earth to eliminate Goku, Piccolo undergoes a transformation himself. He forms a bond with Gohan, becoming his mentor and a vital protector of Earth. Piccolo possesses regenerative abilities, making him a resilient fighter. He is also a master strategist, often coming up with clever plans to defeat stronger opponents. Relies on a combination of martial arts, telekinesis, and energy blasts. Piccolo can also extend his limbs and possesses incredible regenerative abilities, allowing him to recover from severe injuries.

Krillin


Human

Goku's best friend and a skilled Earthling martial artist. Despite not being a Saiyan, Krillin trains relentlessly and plays a crucial role in many battles throughout the series. He is known for his short stature, comedic moments, and unwavering loyalty to his friends. Krillin is a master strategist and utilizes various techniques like the Destructo Disc, a powerful energy cutter. Utilizes a mix of martial arts techniques and energy blasts. Krillin compensates for his lack of raw power with his strategy and mastery of specific techniques like the Destructo Disc, which can potentially cut through anything.

Vegeta


100% Saiyan

The proud prince of the Saiyan race, Vegeta initially arrives on Earth as an antagonist seeking the Dragon Balls for his own conquest. However, his rivalry with Goku pushes him to constantly train and become stronger. Vegeta eventually fights alongside the heroes to protect Earth, becoming a complex anti-hero. He is consumed by his pride and desire to surpass Goku, which fuels his relentless pursuit of power. Similar to Goku, Vegeta relies on hand-to-hand combat with incredible strength and speed. He utilizes his Saiyan energy blasts and unlocks various Super Saiyan transformations throughout the series. Vegeta's fighting style is more aggressive and ruthless than Goku's, driven by his pride and desire to dominate.
